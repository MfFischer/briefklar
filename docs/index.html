How it works

Three steps from
confusion to clarity

No uploads. No accounts. Everything happens on your computer in under 30 seconds.

01
📱
Scan with your phone
Open BriefKlar on your PC. Click "Scan Letter" and scan the QR code with your phone. Photograph the letter — both sides if needed. Phone and PC just need to be on the same WiFi.
02
🔍
AI reads it instantly
BriefKlar runs local OCR on the image, identifies the letter type from 26 categories, extracts the sender, amount, and deadline — applying German legal deadline rules automatically.
03
✅
Know exactly what to do
Get a plain-language summary, step-by-step action list, and "what happens if you ignore this." Set a reminder, generate a reply letter, or export a PDF to share with your advisor.

Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

🔒
100% local. Zero cloud.
Your letters never leave your computer. No server, no account, no subscription. The AI model runs entirely offline using local OCR. Your data is yours — always.
⚖️
Legal deadline intelligence
Automatically applies § 122 AO 3-day delivery presumption and calculates Einspruch windows, payment deadlines, and appeal cutoffs accurately.
✉️
Reply letter templates
Generate formal German reply letters for Einspruch, Widerspruch, Kündigung and more. Add your own Gemini API key for fully AI-drafted replies.
🌍
5 interface languages
Use BriefKlar in English, Deutsch, Türkçe, العربية, or Українська. Designed for expats, immigrants, and anyone navigating Germany from the outside.
📄
PDF export & sharing
Export the full letter analysis as a clean PDF — deadline highlighted, steps numbered — to share with a lawyer, advisor, or family member who can help.
🔔
Deadline reminders
Set native Windows notifications for any deadline. Export to your calendar via .ics file so deadlines never sneak up on you again.
🤝
Free help resources
For each letter type, BriefKlar suggests free help — Mieterverein for rent disputes, Caritas for social benefits, VdK for disability cases, and more.
